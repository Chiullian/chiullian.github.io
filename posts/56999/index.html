[Game with Cards]

​ Alice and Bob play a game. Alice hasnnn cards, theiii-th of them has the integeraia_iai​ written on it. Bob hasmmm cards, thejjj-th of them has the integerbjb_jbj​ written on it.On the first turn of the game, the first player chooses one of his/her cards and puts it on the table (plays it). On the second turn, the second player chooses one of his/her cards such that the integer on it is greater than the integer on the card played on the first turn, and plays it. On the third turn, the first player chooses one of his/her cards such that the integer on it is greater than the integer on the card played on the second turn, and plays it, and so on — the players take turns, and each player has to choose one of his/her cards with greater integer than the card played by the other player on the last turn.If some player cannot make a turn, he/she loses.For example, if Alice has444 cards with numbers[10,5,3,8][10, 5, 3, 8][10,5,3,8], and Bob has333 cards with numbers[6,11,6][6, 11, 6][6,11,6], the game may go as follows: Alice can choose any of her cards. She chooses the card with integer555 and plays it. Bob can choose any of his cards with number greater than555. He chooses a card with integer666 and plays it. Alice can choose any of her cards with number greater than666. She chooses the card with integer101010 and plays it. Bob can choose any of his cards with number greater than101010. He chooses a card with integer111111 and plays it. Alice can choose any of her cards with number greater than111111, but she has no such cards, so she loses. Both Alice and Bob play optimally (if a player is able to win the game no matter how the other player plays, the former player will definitely win the game).You have to answer two questions: who wins if Alice is the first player? who wins if Bob is the first player?

题意：现 A、B 两人各有一些正整数。两人进行博弈：

  • A选择其中一个正整数展示。但是，选择的数字必须严格比上一个被展示的数大。（如果是第一次，则没有限制。）
  • B选择其中一个正整数展示。但是，选择的数字必须严格比上一个被展示的数大。

无法展示正整数的选手失败。对于A先手的情况和B先手的情况，分别输出胜者。

分析:

只需要判断两边那个最大的最大即可,如果相同的话,先手赢
