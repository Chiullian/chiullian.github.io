[Circular Spanning Tree]

​ There arennn nodes arranged in a circle numbered from111 tonnn in the clockwise order. You are also given a binary stringsss of lengthnnn.Your task is to construct a tree on the givennnn nodes satisfying the two conditions below or report that there such tree does not exist: For each nodeiii(1≤i≤n)(1 \le i \le n)(1≤i≤n), the degree of node is even ifsi=0s_i = 0si​=0 and odd ifsi=1s_i = 1si​=1. No two edges of the tree intersect internally in the circle. The edges are allowed to intersect on the circumference. Note that all edges are drawn as straight line segments. For example, edge(u,v)(u, v)(u,v) in the tree is drawn as a line segment connectinguuu andvvv on the circle.A tree onnnn nodes is a connected graph withn−1n - 1n−1 edges.

题意：

给你n个节点

并给出每个节点的度的奇偶性

问你能不能构造出符合要求的树

可以则给出构造方案

先看是否可行,因为肯定要有子节点,所以一定有一个是奇数的节点,树的性质:所有度的和一定是偶数,一条边贡献两个度, 不满足上面就是NO

如何构造: 把所有的为1的当做一个子节点, 然后把后面的0加入到链中,这样就能满足所有的子节点都是奇数, 然后把根节点合并即可.
